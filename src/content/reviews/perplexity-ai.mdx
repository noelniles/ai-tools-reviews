# Perplexity AI Review

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search and research tool that provides sourced, conversational answers to questions.

## Key Features

- Real-time web search integration
- Cited sources for all answers
- Follow-up questions
- Collections for organizing research
- Multiple AI models (GPT-4, Claude, etc.)
- Mobile apps available
- Focus modes (Academic, Writing, etc.)

## Performance

Excellent at research tasks and fact-finding. Provides more reliable information than traditional chatbots by citing sources and searching the web in real-time.

## Pricing

- Free tier with limitations
- Pro: $20/month (600+ searches, GPT-4, Claude 3)

## Pros

- Cites all sources
- Up-to-date information
- Clean, focused interface
- Multiple AI models
- Great for research
- Affordable pricing

## Cons

- Pro tier needed for best models
- Limited searches on free tier
- Can be slower than pure chatbots
- Occasional citation errors
