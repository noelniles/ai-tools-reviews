# Runway ML Review

Runway ML is an AI-powered creative platform specializing in video generation, editing, and effects.

## Key Features

- Text-to-video generation (Gen-2)
- Image-to-video
- Video inpainting and object removal
- Green screen replacement
- Motion tracking
- Slow motion effects
- 30+ AI Magic Tools

## Video Quality

Generates impressive short video clips with good coherence. Best for creative effects and experimental work. Quality improving rapidly with each update.

## Pricing

- Free tier with limited credits
- Standard: $15/month (625 credits)
- Pro: $35/month (2250 credits)
- Unlimited: $95/month

## Pros

- Cutting-edge video AI
- Many creative tools in one platform
- Relatively easy to use
- Regular feature updates
- Good for content creators

## Cons

- Credits burn quickly
- Video length limitations
- Quality inconsistent
- Can be expensive for heavy use
- Learning curve for advanced features
