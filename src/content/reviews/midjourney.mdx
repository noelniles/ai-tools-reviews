- Discord-based interface
- Multiple aspect ratios and styles
- Upscaling and variation generation

- Active community

## Verdict

Midjourney remains the gold standard for AI image generation, particularly for artistic and creative work. The image quality is unmatched, though the Discord interface and lack of granular control may frustrate some users. For anyone serious about AI art, Midjourney is worth the investment.
